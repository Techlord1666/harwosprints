We are assuring you of our product quality which comes from other parts of the world known best manufacturing standard and practice like us. Sometimes, during transit and production in bulk few error occur and we regret to admit but trust us to do a perfect replacement of broken, torn or defective products.

Terms & Conditions

Our refund and returns policy lasts 30 days. If 30 days have passed since your purchase, we can’t offer you a full refund or exchange. To be eligible for a return, your item/s must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging.

Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your returned item. We will also notify you of our commitment to replace a perfect product after we are sure that error is on our part.
