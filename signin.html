The cost of shipping is charged separate from product/s but added to payment which is borne by customer kindly always fill up your delivery address properly in the space provided. Quality of materials

We are assuring you of our product quality which comes from other parts of the world known best manufacturing standard and practice like us. trust us to do a perfect replacement of broken, torn or defective products. please always notify us by mail or chat immediately.

Our refund and returns policy lasts 30 days. To be eligible for a return, your item/s must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging. Refunds are made after a replacement of perfect product has been exhausted.

Exchanges

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. If you need to exchange it for the same item, send us an email at harwosprintsltd@gmail.com and send your item to: harwosprint.ng – 63, Tejuosho Road, Lagos.
